What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive financial and compliance services designed to meet the complex needs of modern businesses operating across India and internationally.

Audit & Assurance

Our audit practice delivers independent, rigorous examination of financial records—providing credibility to your stakeholders and uncovering operational inefficiencies before they become problems.

  • Statutory Audit under the Companies Act, 2013
  • Tax Audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act
  • Internal & Concurrent Audits
  • Bank Branch & Stock Audits
  • Forensic Accounting & Fraud Investigation
  • Due Diligence Audits for M&A transactions

Direct Taxation

Strategic tax planning and full lifecycle compliance services for individuals, corporates, partnerships, and trusts—reducing your effective tax rate while ensuring complete legal compliance.

  • Individual & Corporate ITR Filing
  • Tax Planning & Optimization Strategies
  • TDS Compliance & Return Filing
  • Transfer Pricing Study & Documentation
  • Representation before Assessing Officers & ITAT
  • Tax Litigation & High Court Appeals

GST & Indirect Tax

Expert guidance through the complexities of Goods and Services Tax—from registration to refunds, ensuring accurate compliance and maximum input tax credit utilisation.

  • GST Registration & Amendments
  • Monthly & Quarterly Return Filing (GSTR-1, 3B, 9)
  • GST Annual Return & Reconciliation
  • ITC Audit & Optimisation
  • Customs & Import Duty Advisory
  • Departmental Representation & Notices

Corporate & Secretarial Advisory

End-to-end company law and secretarial compliance services—ensuring your corporate governance meets every regulatory requirement across Company Law, FEMA, and SEBI regulations.

  • Company Incorporation (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC)
  • Annual ROC Filings & Secretarial Compliance
  • FEMA / RBI Compliance for Foreign Investments
  • Board & Committee Meeting Support
  • Winding Up & Dissolution
  • NBFC & Section 8 Company Setup

Virtual CFO Services

Senior financial leadership on a flexible, fractional basis—giving growth-stage companies the strategic finance capability of a full-time CFO at a fraction of the cost.

  • Monthly MIS & KPI Dashboards
  • Cash Flow & Working Capital Management
  • Investor-ready Financial Modelling
  • Fund Raise & Due Diligence Support
  • Cost Optimisation Reviews
  • Board & Investor Reporting

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Reliable, accurate, and timely accounting services—so your books are always audit-ready and your management team always has the numbers they need to make confident decisions.

  • Day-to-day Bookkeeping (Tally, Zoho, QuickBooks)
  • Financial Statement Preparation under IND AS
  • Payroll Processing & Compliance
  • Bank & Account Reconciliations
  • Accounts Receivable / Payable Management
  • Year-end Closing & Finalisation
